Technical Support Programs

From idea to launch — with the technical support you need.

Over 70% of the founders we work with don't have technical backgrounds. They're successfully building tech companies anyway. Whether you're a non-technical visionary needing to bridge skill gaps or a technical founder looking to accelerate development, OM's Tech Support Programs give you the resources and hands-on support to build faster and smarter.

Three ways to get technical help

The programs

Emerging Tech Internship

Connects Opportunity Machine startups with technical students — undergraduate and graduate — through paid internship opportunities, providing the talent needed to develop and expand your product without the traditional barriers of outsourcing development. Students work under the direction of a Technical Expert in Residence or senior tech expert.

Technical Expert in Residence

For startups without a technical founder. Seasoned technical experts help you overcome technology challenges and progress toward product development — gaining clarity on development pathways and the confidence to move forward with a plan to build a viable, scalable product.

Other Partnerships

OM partners with the University of Louisiana at Lafayette to connect startups with technical students through class projects, university-sponsored internships, and other collaborations — hands-on experience for students, accelerated product development for you.

How the pairing actually works

Real students, real product work

Emerging Tech Internship pairs UL Lafayette Computer Science students — juniors and seniors through PhD level — with idea-stage founders who've met the program's requirements to bring their technology to an MVP. Founders get real engineering hands on the product; students get real production work, not a classroom exercise.
