Innovate South

Branded as the "Lafayette Startup Conference | Innovate South." Three days of the regional innovation ecosystem — schedule, speakers, a pitch competition, and networking. April 22–24, 2026.

Meet the right people, on purpose. The stage is built for operators — from zero-to-one builders to scale-stage executives — every speaker has lived the grind, made the tough calls, and shipped outcomes that matter. They'll share what worked, what didn't, and what they'd try again tomorrow.

  • Find your people — whether you're here to connect with other founders, tech talent, or innovation ecosystem supporters, you're in the right place.
  • Make real connections — work the room with like-minded innovators who are building, scaling, or exploring what's next.
  • Walk away energized — leave with momentum: new contacts, actionable insights, and the next step already mapped out.
