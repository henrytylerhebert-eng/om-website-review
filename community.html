Mix & Jingle

Pitch practice with a room that moves.

Opportunity Machine runs Mix & Jingle for OM members who need reps outside a slide deck. Builder 1.0 founders work the room for 40 minutes, pitch one conversation at a time, and meet hidden Mystery Judges along the way.

To qualify, founders complete Builder 1.0 and 15 customer discovery meetings. That means the pitch starts with customer evidence, not guesswork.

Use Mix & Jingle to test your message, meet mentors, and learn which parts of your story people repeat.

Startup Circle

Startup Circle

Building a company is lonely work — Startup Circle is the antidote.

It's Opportunity Machine's quarterly gathering for founders in the community to check in with each other: swap wins, talk through what's hard, and find out who else in the ecosystem is tackling something similar.

No agenda to perform, no application to fill out — OM staff send out invites ahead of each session, so if you're part of the community, you'll hear about it.

Watch your inbox for the next invite, or reach out to your OM staff contact in the meantime.
