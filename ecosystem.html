A statewide partnership

OM is now part of Louisiana's official innovation network.

Two state agencies within the Office of Louisiana Economic Development — Louisiana Innovation (LA.IO) and the Louisiana Technology Transfer Office (LTTO) — have teamed up with the Lafayette-based Opportunity Machine to expand startup opportunities across Acadiana. The partnership lets OM leverage LA.IO's open-source platform and LTTO's expertise in navigating non-dilutive federal funding for small businesses, strengthening OM's role as a catalyst for entrepreneurship and growth across the region.

"We recognize that for most first-time founders, our network is their network, which is why this partnership is so critical," said OM Director Destin Ortego. "It's a direct pipeline connecting the grit and ingenuity of Acadiana's entrepreneurs to a powerful statewide platform, giving them access to the investors, grant funding, talent, and customers they need to thrive. It ensures a great idea in Lafayette has the same shot at success as one anywhere in the country."

Announcement copy and Destin Ortego quote per an Acadiana Advocate article, as supplied by Tyler, 2026-07-02 — see claim.laio-ltto-om-partnership.
